Questions I have

[[questions, personal thoughts]]

Why is everything turning into walled gardens? Why is general computing dying? Is it primarily due to the trading of control for convenience or something else?

Internet

  • Internet has got this sucky feeling. Why does the internet suck so much?
  • Why can't I reliably send a lot of files to someone without a middleman and without having any technical knowledge?
  • Why can websites get blocked so easily? Why is everything a slave to embargoes etc?
  • Why are ip blocks still so coveted?
  • Why is information still so much siloed out? Why it's so greatly unbundled and why it fails horribly(financially) when bundled up?
  • Why can't I easily combine a number of sources(websites) into a personal context and then view/use them?
  • Why did the explorability of the web lose itself due to searchability? Why can't I find more unique websites after search has become so much easier?
  • Why can't a city buy cheap boxes and set up it'sown network overnight? Why can't I power a simple box and reliably join the network mby myself without paying up a fuckton of money? Is the internet even decentalized?

Tools of viewing information

  • Why are physical books so much easier to skim thank ebooks? WHy do digital ways of exploration(scrolling etc) suck so much?
