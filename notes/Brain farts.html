What advantage does AI have over humans?

  • Mush much bigger working memory: process without the need of relying on unconscious thought like we humans do
  • Unquestioned synchronicity and communication

What is AI

It's simply software with the ability to generate/build up its own understanding.

Rules

  • Rules are for problems the system cannot control or doesn't understand
  • Needing a rule is a failure of the system.
  • Ideal systems are those where there is no possibility of an error.
  • But such systems are only good in software where states are discrete. Not in real life.
  • In real life, such systems rob away all freedom.

Media industrial complex

Surviving because of mental wars

Progress

  • Progress moves like pushing a bench ahead, one side at a time
  • Capital can make the 2 sides push in sync and speed up the process
  • Otherwise, when one side is pushed ahead, the other side goes a little back(abcktracks), before it's pushed ahead next
  • Like left and right alternation in politics

The waves below waves

  • Centralization-decentralication cycles are the smaller ripples on the waves riding the underlying bigger wave cycle of biological and electronic technology
  • All electronic technology will again converge into us remaking biological things. Maybe that'll end our species and start the next cycle.

What a visionary looks like

  • Imagine people sitting in rows and columns, each person calculating stuff, essentially as a cell
  • When viewed from a macro scale, this looked like a spreadsheet software, leading to its creation
  • A person gotta look at the wave below the waves to truly understand the direction
  • The truly difficult work is throwing a giant new rock to get new waves started

Human brain technical debt

  • The interior layers near the brain stem are huge technical debts
  • The outer layers may provide patches, but the fundamental problems will forever exist (unless it's rebuilt form the ground up)
  • Thus the psychology-hackers (aka attention management scientists) have exploits that will work forever.
  • It's not a solveable problem at this point. It's like as if ther kernel itself is faulty.

When I write things down...

  • I just want to write the thing down. I don't want to tag it, or classify it into files.
  • I don't want to think beyond the content.
  • All I want it a giant file with highlights and accessible search.
  • I will classify and tag when I have the time to organise thought.

Happiness

  • Happiness is a personal reaction to something. It cannot be given from outside.
  • The thing on the outside can only create circumstances to which you react with happiness
